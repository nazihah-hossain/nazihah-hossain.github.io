Featured project

Systems Analysis & Design

Call Management CRM Integration

The University of Texas at Dallas — September – December 2025 • Group 4 of 5 members

Capital Stack, a pay-per-call marketing firm, was capped at ~$60,000/month in revenue due to manual CRM updates, high-volume communication tasks, and fragmented billing processes. Our team designed the Capital Stack Operations Agent — an AI-driven automation system integrating WavvDialer, GoHighLevel CRM, Slack, and Stripe — to eliminate repetitive operational tasks and scale the business without adding headcount.

Business problem

The client could only manage 1,500–2,000 leads/month. Manual CRM entry, untracked billing, and fragmented communications prevented growth. The goal was to double lead capacity while keeping team size constant.

What we built
Requirements & Feasibility
Conducted full technical, economic, and organizational feasibility analysis. Defined 9 functional requirements and 4 categories of non-functional requirements covering performance, security, and compliance.
UML System Design
Produced Use Case Diagrams, Activity Diagrams, System Sequence Diagrams, a State Machine Diagram, and Domain Model & Design Class Diagrams using Lucidchart to model system behavior end-to-end.
Financial Modeling
Built a 5-year NPV/ROI model in Excel. Projected $500K in Year 1 benefits against $21,900 in costs, with break-even in under one month and a cumulative NPV of $2.16M over 5 years.
Agile Project Management
Managed the project using Agile/Scrum with a Gantt chart across 4 phases: Research & Setup, Planning & Design, Integration, and Testing & Deployment — all within a one-month timeline.
UI Storyboard
Designed a 5-screen UI storyboard illustrating the SDR workflow: login, dashboard navigation, date/time selection, lead entry, and appointment confirmation with automated email trigger.
Testing Plan
Developed integration and system test cases for the appointment confirmation and billing workflows, including pre/post-conditions, expected system responses, and Slack bot validation steps.
